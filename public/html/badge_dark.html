Badge

v2.1.0

Visual indicator for status, labels, and numeric counts. Designed for high-density interfaces with support for multiple variants and sizes.

Interactive Playground

System Operational

Installation

Terminal
npm install @agency/ui

Variants

Use the variant prop to control the visual style of the badge to match its semantic meaning.

Neutral Outline

Neutral

Used for general labels or tags.

Success Online

Success

Indicates a positive state or successful action.

Warning warning Review Needed

Warning

Indicates caution or a non-blocking issue.

Error block Failed

Destructive

Indicates a critical error or destructive state.

Information v2.0 Beta Solid Primary

Info & Brand

Used for neutral information or highlighting brand-related features.
